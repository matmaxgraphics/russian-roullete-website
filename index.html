Gameplay

Russian Roulette is played virtually and hosted by a telegram bot. On a high-level, players can play the game as follows:

1

Join the telegram community first and acquire some $BULLETs on any dex of your choice on Solana

2

Also, initiate the game bot on Telegram in a private chat using /start to acquire a secure wallet address.

3

Return to telegram community and start a new game or join an already existing game, then place your bet

4

Start taking turns to pull the trigger or pass the revolver to another player until one player is eliminated.

5

Emerge as one of the winners and get a share of the eliminated players’ tokens. Highest bet gets the highest share.
